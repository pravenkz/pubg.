Pubg tournament open to all

We are organizing a pubg tournament which is open to all category, The tournament will take place in one day.

There will three rounds for the solo event.

Knock-out round

  • 50 participants in one room
  • which will be conducted twice for different participants
  • Top 18 qualifies for final

Last chance qualifiers

  • The remaning who did'nt qualify will get a last chance in last qualifiers round
  • The qualifiers round will consist of participants eliminated from 1st round
  • top 4 qualifies for final

Final round

  • Final round will consist of 36 participants qualified from the first round
  • Including 4 participants which gets qualified from the last qualifiers round
  • Which concludes to 40 participants for final round

Duo

  • 25 teams in a room
  • Final round
  • Top 3 teams wins

All Girls round

  • 15-20 participants
  • Final round
  • winner takes all

Entry fee and prize money

100 per head for solo

200 for duo

Prize money

3000 1st place (solo)

2000 2nd place(solo)

1000 3rd place (solo)

4000 1st place (duo)

2000 2nd place (duo)

1500 1st place (all girls)

The prize money may vary depending upon the number of participants

If all the slots are filled the price money will remain same
